Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a hazard?

Tap the "Report" tab at the bottom of the app, take a photo, select the severity level, add notes, and tap "Submit Hazard Report".

How do I assign a hazard to someone?

Only supervisors can assign hazards. Go to the "Hazards" tab, tap on a hazard, select a maintenance user from the dropdown, set a due date, and tap "Assign".

I'm not receiving push notifications. What should I do?

Make sure you've granted notification permissions to the app in your device settings. Go to Settings → Hazard Hub → Notifications and ensure they're enabled.

How do I invite team members?

Supervisors can invite users from the "Team" tab. Tap "Invite User", enter their email, select their role, and tap "Send Invite". They'll receive an email with a code to join.

Can I edit a hazard report after submitting?

Currently, hazard reports cannot be edited after submission. If you need to make changes, please contact your supervisor or create a new report with updated information.

How do I mark a hazard as fixed?

Once you've completed the work on an assigned hazard, open the hazard detail page, optionally upload a "fix photo" showing the completed work, and tap "Mark Fixed". A supervisor will then verify and close the hazard.

Where can I view generated reports?

Go to the "Reports" tab to view all generated PDF reports. Tap on any report to open it. You can also share reports from this screen.

What are the different user roles?

Supervisor: Can assign hazards, invite users, manage team, and verify completed work.
Maintenance: Can report hazards and mark assigned hazards as fixed.
Worker: Can report hazards and view hazards assigned to them.
Safety Lead: Similar to supervisor, with additional safety oversight responsibilities.

Technical Issues

If you're experiencing technical problems:

  • Try signing out and signing back in
  • Make sure you have the latest version of the app
  • Check your internet connection
  • Restart the app

If the problem persists, please contact support with details about the issue, including any error messages you see.
